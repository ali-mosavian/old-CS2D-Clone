* AS (Assassination)  
 
Terrorists: Try to kill the VIP
Counter-Terrorists: Protect the VIP
VIP (Counter-Terrorist): Reach the Escape Point  


* CS (Hostage Rescue)  
 
Terrorists: Protect the Hostages
Counter-Terrorists: Rescue the Hostages  


* DE (Bomb/Defuse) 
 
Terrorists: Plant the Bomb
Counter-Terrorists: Protect the Bombspot(s) / defuse the Bomb
